How scoring works

VALTRACK builds a combat score every round, then averages and curves it into a 0–1000 performance index.

  • Kills — base value scales with victim ACS (+ eco damage bonus if you hurt their next buy).
  • Clutches — 1.5× kill value when you win a 1vN after teammates are down.
  • First blood — +25 pts · First death — −35 pts.
  • Assist damage — useful non-kill damage (discounted in hopeless lost rounds).
Avg = Raw total ÷ Rounds Score = (Avg ÷ 45)0.98 × 1000
